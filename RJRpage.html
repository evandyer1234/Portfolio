Rocket Jump Racing

Race to achieve victory in this action packed, explosive themed racing game! Rocket Jump Racing is a competitive explosive themed shooter where the players must shoot rockets to propel themselves forward to cross the finish line first. There are three playable characters: The Dictator, a militaristic potato, Chappie, a Buff Pineapple from the Pineapple Hive-Mind, and Sasha, a Rhino hiding away from poachers.
