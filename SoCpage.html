Spark of Chaos

Spark of chaos was a short, solo Game Jam project, that went thorugh a few iterations afterward. It is a twin stick shooter where the player muat survev as long as they can, racking up points by killing enemies and destroying "Order Generators". Each generator destroyed adds a randomly generated line of text, showing what chaos the player has introduced into the world.

The Jam's theme was RNG. The randomness of this game comes from an affect that randomly changes whenever the player reloads their guns. Each gun has a random number attached to it, the twi numbers added together is the chosen effect of the bullets on hit. The higher the number, the better the effect.
