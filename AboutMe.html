About Me

Hello there! I'm Evan and I like to play and make video games. I tend to sway towards the comedic side of things. My sense of humor, which is a staple among most of my projects, is ever evolving. As a result of that, each game has a unique sense of humor with different types of jokes.

Even when the goal is to make a demo to show off what I've learned, I almost always end up going above and beyond to add as much content as I can to make the experience as complete as can be. I always look at my work not as a bunch of demos, but a bunch of games, albeit short ones.
