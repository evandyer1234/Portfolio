BTCOOEOR

BTCOOEOR stands for "Beat the Crap Out of Each Other Racing". It features 4 player multiplayer, 10 levels ending in a boss fight, a tutorial and a super secret level. The players need to jump, punch, and self-destruct their way to victory. Each level implements a new gimmick, for example, there are spining platforms, windmill platforms, kill blocks, trampolines, and platforms that hit you with a brick if you stand on them for too long. Mastering all of these elements will be necessary to take on the final boss, the announcer himself. The Announcer pilots a giant mech suit that fires lasers and bombs at the players. The players need to put aside their differences to defeat him by taking down his generators.
