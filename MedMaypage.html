Medieval Mayhem

Medieval Mayhem sees the player cut the line at a medieval-themed amusement park. They need to take out security gaurds and workers dressed up as knights to make to to final boss: a dragon.

For this project I was in charge of making the enemies and the boss fight. The boss fight is what I am especially proud of, a lot of work went into it and it took a lot longer than the other enemies to make. I managed to cram in two phases, the second phase making confetti fire shoot from either end of the stage and that dragon's attacks get more challenging.
